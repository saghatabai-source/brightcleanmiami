How Much Does House Cleaning Cost in Miami?

The cost of house cleaning services in Miami depends on several factors including home size, cleaning type and frequency.

Average Cleaning Prices in Miami

On average, professional house cleaning services in Miami cost between $120 and $300 depending on the size of the home and the level of cleaning required.

Factors That Affect Cleaning Prices

  • Size of the home
  • Type of cleaning service
  • Number of rooms and bathrooms
  • Frequency of cleaning

Apartment Cleaning Costs

Apartment cleaning in Miami typically costs between $100 and $180 depending on the size and condition of the apartment.

Deep Cleaning Services

Deep cleaning services require more time and effort, which increases the price compared to regular maintenance cleaning.
