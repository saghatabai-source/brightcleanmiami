Ultimate Apartment Cleaning Checklist in Miami, FL

A complete room-by-room professional cleaning guide for Miami residents.

Keeping your apartment clean in Miami’s humid climate requires consistency and attention to detail. Whether you live in Brickell, Miami Beach, or Coral Gables, this professional apartment cleaning checklist will help you maintain a spotless and healthy living environment.

Kitchen Cleaning Checklist

  • Wipe countertops and backsplash
  • Clean sink and disinfect faucet
  • Wipe exterior of cabinets
  • Clean stovetop and control knobs
  • Microwave interior and exterior
  • Refrigerator exterior (interior monthly deep clean)
  • Sweep and mop floors

Bathroom Cleaning Checklist

  • Scrub shower walls and glass doors
  • Disinfect toilet (seat, base, and handle)
  • Clean mirrors and vanity
  • Wipe countertops and sinks
  • Empty trash bins
  • Mop floors

Bedroom Cleaning Checklist

  • Dust furniture and nightstands
  • Change bed linens
  • Vacuum carpets or mop floors
  • Wipe baseboards
  • Organize closets

Living Room Cleaning Checklist

  • Dust all surfaces and electronics
  • Vacuum sofa and rugs
  • Clean glass tables
  • Wipe light switches and door handles
  • Vacuum or mop floors

Miami-Specific Cleaning Tips

Miami’s humidity can lead to faster dust accumulation and mold growth. We recommend weekly maintenance cleaning and monthly deep cleaning to prevent moisture-related damage.
